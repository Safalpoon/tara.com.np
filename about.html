About Me

My Journey

I'm Tara Gurung, a Software and DevOps Engineer with a passion for automating and optimizing software development processes. My journey in technology began over 8 years ago when I discovered my fascination with building and deploying applications.

Throughout my career, I've worked with various organizations to implement DevOps practices that have significantly improved their development workflows, reduced time to market, and enhanced overall software quality.

I believe in the power of continuous learning and sharing knowledge with the community. This has led me to speak at various tech meetups and conferences, where I enjoy connecting with like-minded professionals and discussing the latest trends in DevOps.

My Expertise

Infrastructure as Code

Expertise in Terraform, CloudFormation, and Ansible for automating infrastructure provisioning and configuration management.

CI/CD Pipelines

Proficient in designing and implementing continuous integration and delivery pipelines using Jenkins, GitHub Actions, and GitLab CI.

Container Orchestration

Skilled in Docker and Kubernetes for containerization and orchestration of microservices architectures.

My Approach

I approach DevOps with a focus on three core principles:

  • Automation: Automating repetitive tasks to increase efficiency and reduce human error.
  • Collaboration: Breaking down silos between development and operations teams to foster better communication and shared responsibility.
  • Continuous Improvement: Constantly evaluating and refining processes to achieve better results over time.

This approach has helped my clients achieve significant improvements in their software development lifecycle, resulting in faster deployments, more reliable systems, and happier development teams.
